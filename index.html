Dedicated to protecting everyday drivers and their property

About Us

At Flood Finder, our mission is to safeguard communities from the perils of flooded roads. We are committed to utilizing cutting-edge technology, including cameras powered by state-of-the-art neural networks to detect and alert the public about hazardous road conditions. Our goal is to significantly reduce the damage to vehicles and, most importantly, protect people from harm caused by flooding.

Learn more

Our Technology

At Flood Finder, we leverage the power of advanced neural network technology integrated into our state-of-the-art camera systems. These cameras are strategically positioned to monitor and analyze road conditions in real-time, enabling us to detect flood risks with unparalleled accuracy. Our innovative software processes data instantaneously, ensuring timely alerts and updates are sent to users. By combining AI insights with real-world observations, we provide a robust solution for flood detection and prevention, keeping communities safe and informed.

Learn more

Why Choose Us?

Choosing Flood Finder means partnering with a leader in flood detection and safety innovation. Our commitment to community safety drives us to develop and implement the most reliable and effective flood detection systems. With Flood Finder, you gain peace of mind knowing that you're protected by cutting-edge technology designed to forecast and alert you of flood threats before they impact your safety. Our dedication to excellence, combined with our proactive approach to flood management, makes us the trusted choice for communities and individuals alike.
